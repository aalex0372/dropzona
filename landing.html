How DROPZONE Works

Three simple steps — from a streamer's kill to a skin in your Steam inventory.

STEP 01
Streamer Sets Triggers
The streamer chooses which CS2 events activate drops — kills, aces, bomb defuses, round wins — and loads skins into their drop pool.
STEP 02
Game Events Fire
DROPZONE watches the live game via CS2 Game State Integration. When a trigger event happens, a drop is instantly activated.
STEP 03
Viewer Wins Skins
A random eligible viewer is selected as the winner. They receive an automatic Steam trade offer — accept it, and the skin is yours.
